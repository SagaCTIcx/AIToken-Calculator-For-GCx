Genesys Cloud AI Token Pricing Calculators

Simple reference calculators for estimating Genesys Cloud AI token consumption and monthly cost for different AI-related capabilities, including bots, virtual agents, agentic virtual agents, AI Guides, analytics, copilot, messaging, and related services.

About this page

This repository contains two versions of the same AI token pricing calculator: one in USD and one in EUR. The purpose is to make it easier to estimate potential monthly token consumption when discussing Genesys Cloud AI use cases with customers, internal teams, or partners.

These calculators are intended as a practical estimation aid. They do not replace official commercial quotes, contractual terms, or final confirmation from Genesys. Official information about Genesys Cloud tokens model is avialable at href=https://help.genesys.cloud/articles/genesys-cloud-tokens-model/

Important: The calculators were prepared to reflect the newer pricing approach, including May 14, 2026 updates such as Agentic Virtual Agent session pricing and the change in treatment of AI Guides creation.

Basic explanation of Genesys Cloud AI token usage

Genesys Cloud AI capabilities can consume tokens in different ways depending on the type of service being used. In simplified terms, token consumption may be based on:

  • minutes, for example voice bot usage
  • sessions, for example digital bot or virtual agent usage
  • users, for example analytics or copilot features
  • events or messages, for example predictive engagement or social / WhatsApp messaging
  • creation of assets, historically relevant for AI Guides

A practical architectural point is that not all self-service or AI-enabled interactions are charged in the same way. The consumption model depends on whether the solution uses:

  • classic bot flows
  • virtual agents
  • agentic virtual agents
  • other AI services such as summaries, translation, scoring, analytics, or copilot

In addition, where multiple AI resources are used in a single interaction, Genesys documentation indicates that billing logic may follow the highest relevant AI resource used for that interaction. Because of that, solution design has a direct impact on token estimation.

Pricing context

The two calculators below are separated only by currency:

  • USD calculator for teams discussing pricing in US dollars
  • EUR calculator for teams discussing pricing in euros

Each calculator includes the same usage categories, but the estimated monthly cost is displayed in the selected currency. The EUR version uses the EUR token price assumption you requested.

Recommendation: use the calculators as a pre-sales and architecture estimation tool, then validate assumptions against the official Genesys commercial model and latest documentation.
